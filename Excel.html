Comparing the costs of products in different marts

Comparing the costs of products in different markets using Excel involves collecting data on the prices of products across multiple markets, then organizing and analyzing the data in Excel to determine which market offers the best value for the products of interest. This can be done by creating a table in Excel with the product names and their corresponding prices in each mart. The data can then be organized using pivot tables, and charts such as bar charts or line charts can be created to visualize the comparison. To make an informed decision, various Excel functions, such as MIN, MAX, AVERAGE, and SUM, can be used to calculate the average, minimum, and maximum prices, and determine the mart with the most favorable prices for the products.
