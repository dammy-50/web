Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is a process of analyzing and summarizing data to identify patterns and relationships within the data. In Python, the commonly used libraries for EDA include pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn. Pandas is used to clean and manipulate data, matplotlib is used for plotting, and seaborn provides advanced visualization capabilities.
